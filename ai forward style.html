Context:

This design system supports AI education initiatives across 28 school districts in Orange County, CA. The audience includes district leaders, principals, teachers, students, and small organizations building programs. The visual system must balance institutional credibility with creative innovation and clarity for complex AI concepts.

• Personality: Minimalist, intelligent, calm confidence, slightly bold when highlighting innovation

• Primary emotion: Trust first, curiosity second

• Tone: Human-centered, educator-friendly, quietly sophisticated

• Favor: whitespace, precision, thoughtful hierarchy, accessible color contrast
